## How to Fill Out Dates

### For the Next Week
```
2025-11-09	morning	[Your message]
2025-11-09	afternoon	[Your message]
2025-11-09	evening	[Your message]
2025-11-10	morning	[Your message]
...
```

### For the Next Month
Create all combinations of dates and parts (morning/afternoon/evening) for 30 days ahead.

### Pro Tip: Use Google Sheets Formulas
To auto-generate dates:
1. In cell A2, enter: `=TODAY()`
2. In cell A3, enter: `=A2+1`
3. Drag down to auto-fill sequential dates
